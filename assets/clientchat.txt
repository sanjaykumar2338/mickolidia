Trader Consistency Rule – Implementation Brief

Purpose:
This system is designed to control payouts for traders according to the “consistency rule.” The rule ensures that profits are spread across multiple trading days and prevents traders from generating most of their profit in a single day, which could risk the company’s cash flow.

⸻

1. What the rule is
	•	Consistency Rule: No more than 40% of total account profit can come from a single trading day.
	•	If a trader exceeds this limit, the excess profit is not eligible for payout until additional trading days are completed.
	•	This protects the company and ensures payouts remain sustainable and progressive.

⸻

2. Where it appears
	1.	Terms & Conditions: Already included.
	2.	Payout Policy: Add this line:
“Payout eligibility requires compliance with the consistency rule, ensuring profits are distributed across multiple trading days.”
